The Alchemist’s Cave Tattoo studio has been in Easter Road since 2008. It is managed by Julian Moray whose career as a tattoo artist started back in the year 2000.

Julian is a conscious tattoo artist who brings his philosophy to the studio and his work, giving him the edge on competition.
Julian believes in building a close relationship with his customers that allows him to understand their needs and facilitates the flow of ideas.

“Tattoos and body art are a way to express oneself and inking your skin is a personal experience that will remain with you for years to come”.

You can book an appointment over the phone or through an email but a visit to the studio is the best way to initiate the process, discuss ideas and work with you on your desired tattoo design.

Please note we can only provide you with a quote after your first visit to the studio and once your requirements and expectations are discussed with us. Social media is a good way to get an idea of the studio’s work but is not a reliable way of communication since during busy times the wait for a response might be lengthy.

Deposits are non-refundable and all changes to a tattoo design must be submitted to the studio 48h before your session.
